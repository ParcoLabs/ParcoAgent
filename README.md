Parco Property Management Agent

The Parco Property Management Agent is a service designed to assist property managers and provide transparency for investors. It takes on the operational work that is often time-consuming or overlooked, ensuring that tenant and vendor interactions are handled consistently.

Features

Tenant communication: Sends rent reminders, late notices, and issue follow-ups via email or SMS.

Vendor management: Contacts vendors, tracks job progress, and verifies that work is completed.

Payment tracking: Ensures vendors are paid only after verified work, with logs available for reporting.

SLA monitoring: Tracks service level agreements, flags overdue tasks, and escalates when needed.

Issue resolution: Processes maintenance requests from intake through to completion.

Operational insights: Identifies recurring issues, cost overruns, and vendor performance trends.
